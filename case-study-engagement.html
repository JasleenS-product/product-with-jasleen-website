Case Study

Boosting Post-Purchase Engagement

Transforming Customer Engagement through WhatsApp Business API

The Challenge

Our client, a leading e-commerce platform, struggled with maintaining customer engagement after purchase. Despite having a large customer base, they faced declining renewal rates and missed opportunities for cross-selling. Traditional email follow-ups weren't cutting it anymore.

The Solution

We implemented a comprehensive post-purchase engagement strategy leveraging WhatsApp Business API:

  • Personalized order updates and delivery tracking
  • Interactive product usage guides and tips
  • Automated follow-up system with personalized recommendations
  • Direct channel for customer support and feedback

The Impact

45% Increase in engagement
35% Higher renewal rates
28% Cross-selling success

Key Takeaway

Meeting customers where they are - on WhatsApp - and providing value through personalized engagement creates lasting relationships and drives business growth.

Project Overview

  • Duration: 4 months
  • Industry: E-commerce
  • Focus Area: Customer Engagement
